Creeds Overview

Creeds and confessions have played a vital role in defining and defending the core beliefs of Christianity throughout history. They serve as concise summaries of faith, often formulated in response to theological controversies or heresies. These documents not only provide a foundation for doctrinal unity but also offer believers a means to articulate their faith clearly and confidently. In this collection, you will find some of the most significant creeds and confessions that have shaped Christian theology and practice over the centuries. -AI
