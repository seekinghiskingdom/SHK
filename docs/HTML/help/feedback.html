We value your feedback and suggestions to help us improve Seeking His Kingdom. Whether you have ideas for new features, notice any issues, or simply want to share your experience using the site, we would love to hear from you.

How to Provide Feedback

You can share your feedback and suggestions through the following channels:

  • Contact Page: Use our contact page to send us a message directly.
  • Feedback Form: Submit your thoughts via our quick feedback form.

What can you give feedback on?

To help us address your feedback effectively, we have outlined some areas you might consider:

  • Page 1: Info & Overall Experience - Share your overall impressions, ease of navigation, and usability.
  • Page 2: General Feedback - Provide specific comments on content quality, design, and features.
  • Page 3: Bugs & Errors - Report any technical issues or bugs you encounter while using the site.
  • Page 4: Feature Requests - Suggest new features or improvements you would like to see.
  • Page 5: Content Suggestions - Recommend topics or resources you believe would enhance our offerings.
  • Page 6: Design & Usability - Offer feedback on the visual design and user experience.
  • Page 7: Navigation & Structure - Comment on the organization and structure of the site.
  • Page 8: Accessibility - Share your thoughts on how accessible the site is for all users.
  • Page 9: Tools - Provide feedback on any tools or interactive features available on the site.
  • Page 10: Games - Suggest improvements or new ideas for games related to biblical learning.
  • Page 11: Personal Feedback - Share user-specific feedback or testimonials about your experience.
